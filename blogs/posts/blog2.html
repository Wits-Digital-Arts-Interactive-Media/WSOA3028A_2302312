Week 1 - 16/02/2024: wireframes and inspiration

I created the first rough wireframes for the project this week. I wanted the website to have a pretty standard layout so as not to confuse or intimidate any users. When looking for inspiration, I saw many websites that had users interacting with them in ways not unlike a video game. One made users drive a little car around in a world and each location was a piece of the website. another was a linear sidescroller that had users control a little character running through the website. These websites were very impressive and innovative, I love how they challenged the normal idea of website navigation. However, I think for a portfolio website, I don't want to scare off potential employers/clients because they were too overwhelmed with an unfamiliar way of navigating the web. As such, I have decided on the standard home bar at the top of the screen that collapses into a smaller version when users scroll down. I want my website to feel easy to navigate, but I still want some gems of originality sprinkled around it. These should be non essential to the flow of the site but still a treat to find. I'm not sure what these 'gems' will be yet though.
