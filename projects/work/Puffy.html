Puffy Anarchist

A 2D platformer all about breaking unfair rules

A game where players play as a cast-out puffball. Blatantly fighting against the rules is the only way to move forward

This Game was made as an entry to the 2023 Campus Game Jam. The theme was "Anarchy"

This project required me to work incredibly fast and efficiently as we were only a team of two and the time limit with these jams are rather strict. I developed the backend, and my partner did the front end.
